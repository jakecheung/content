Zhaoqing Day Trip Itinerary

Depart from Guangzhou, Ziyun Valley Experience + Specialty Café Leisure Time

10:00 - 12:30
Departure & Arrival
Guangzhou → Ziyun Valley, Zhaoqing
  • 10:00-10:15 Gather and depart from Guangzhou (Suggested locations: near Tiyu Xilu, Gongyuanqian subway stations)
  • 10:15-12:00 Travel to Ziyun Valley in Zhaoqing (Recommended: charter a minibus for comfort and convenience)
  • 12:00-12:30 Arrive at restaurant and prepare for lunch
Transportation Suggestion: For a group of 11 people, we strongly recommend chartering a minibus - it's the most comfortable, convenient, and cost-effective option.
12:30 - 13:30
Lunch
Restaurants near the scenic area
  • Option 1: Quick lunch at Wenshu Village or Houli Pier area near Ziyun Valley exit (farmhouse cuisine)
  • Option 2: "Nanhai Pigeon World" at Beiling Mountain, not far from the city center, with high ratings
  • Option 3: Cantonese dim sum (Zhonghua Tea House, Ziyue Li Tea House)
Recommendation: Fresh river food or Cantonese dim sum, choose based on the group's taste preferences.
13:30 - 14:00
Travel to Ziyun Valley Scenic Area
Restaurant → Ziyun Valley Scenic Area
Transportation: Travel from lunch location to Ziyun Valley scenic area, approximately 30 minutes.
14:00 - 17:00
Ziyun Valley Visit
Ziyun Valley Scenic Area
  • 14:00-14:15 River crossing & entry: Take a boat across the river from the pier, enjoy the scenery of Lingyang Gorge on the Xijiang River
  • 14:15-14:45 Cultural exploration & photos: Visit the Duan Inkstone Old Pit site, take photos at the Chain Bridge and Fengyu Bridge
  • 14:45-16:15 Core stream trekking experience: Walk upstream along the stream, pass by Tianqing Pond and other pools, experience the cool stream water
  • 16:15-17:00 Return journey & rest: Return to the area near the entrance
Photo Tip: The Chain Bridge and Fengyu Bridge are very photogenic and perfect for group photos.
17:00 - 17:30
Travel to Specialty Café
Ziyun Valley → Specialty Café
Transportation: Travel from Ziyun Valley to the specialty café, approximately 30 minutes.
17:30 - 18:30
Specialty Café Break
Specialty Café in Zhaoqing
  • 17:30-18:30 Enjoy specialty coffee and desserts, relax during leisurely time
Recommended Cafés: Zhaoqing has several specialty cafés with elegant environments, such as "Xinghu Xiaozhu" and "Yanqian Coffee." Choose cafés with lake or mountain views to allow the team to relax in a comfortable environment.
18:30 - 20:30
Return Journey
Zhaoqing → Guangzhou
  • 18:30-20:30 Return to Guangzhou
Reminder: Expected arrival in Guangzhou around 20:30, ending the pleasant day trip.
